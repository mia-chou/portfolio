Project Overview

The problem space

This project explores how improving transparency in student accommodation contracts can reduce disputes and increase trust among international students in the UK. From contract language to refund timelines, small clarity gaps create significant anxiety.

⚡ Interactive Prototype — Contract Clarity Viewer
Original Contract Language

The Licensor hereby reserves the right to terminate this agreement with immediate effect upon breach of any condition herein, including but not limited to non-payment of the licence fee within the stipulated period, without prejudice to any claim for damages or outstanding monies owed by the Licensee to the Licensor.

⚠️ 78% of international students said they did not fully understand this clause.
Plain English Version

If you don't pay your rent on time, or break any of the rules in this contract, we can end your tenancy immediately. We may also ask you to pay any money you still owe us.

💡 Key point: Pay on time to avoid losing your accommodation.
02

Research Insights

What we discovered

📄

Impenetrable Legal Language

International students struggle to interpret contract terms written in dense legal English.

⏳

Invisible Refund Timelines

Refund timelines lack visibility, creating anxiety and eroding trust during vulnerable moments.

🔍

No Status Tracking

There is no mechanism to track an ongoing dispute or refund request, leaving students in limbo.

📉

Trust Erosion

Communication gaps increase perceived risk, reducing brand trust and making students feel unprotected.

Opportunities

Where we can intervene

1
Make contract language clearer and more accessible
Plain English summaries, glossaries, and highlighted key clauses
2
Provide visible process timelines
Show students exactly where they are in the refund or move-in process
3
Enable students to track their refund or dispute status
Real-time updates reduce anxiety and inbound support calls
