Project Overview

The problem space

Opening a bank account should feel empowering — but for many new customers, it's overwhelming. This project redesigns the onboarding journey to be transparent, guided, and reassuring, reducing drop-offs and building long-term trust from the very first interaction.

02

Research Insights

What we discovered

📋

Unclear Document Requirements

Customers don't know what documents they need upfront, causing frustration and mid-flow drop-offs.

🕐

Invisible Application Status

Once submitted, applicants have no visibility into where their application stands or how long it will take.

😰

Fear of Rejection

Uncertainty around eligibility deters vulnerable and first-time customers from even starting the process.

🗣️

Jargon-Heavy Language

Legal and financial terminology alienates new customers, especially those unfamiliar with UK banking.

Opportunities

1
Set clear expectations before the process begins

Make the application journey visible at every step

3
Communicate decisions with clarity and empathy
